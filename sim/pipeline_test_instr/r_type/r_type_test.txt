========================================
RISC-V R-Type指令测试程序
========================================
功能: 测试所有R-Type算术逻辑指令
      ADD, SUB, SLL, SLT, SLTU, XOR, SRL, SRA, OR, AND
========================================

地址    | 机器码      | 指令                    | 说明
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === 初始化寄存器 ===
0x00    | 0x00A00093  | ADDI x1, x0, 10        | x1 = 10  (0b01010)
0x04    | 0x00F00113  | ADDI x2, x0, 15        | x2 = 15  (0b01111) - 用于AND测试
0x08    | 0x01400193  | ADDI x3, x0, 20        | x3 = 20  (0b10100)
0x0c    | 0x00400213  | ADDI x4, x0, 4         | x4 = 4   (移位量)
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === R-Type算术测试 ===
0x10    | 0x00308233  | ADD x5, x1, x3         | x5 = 10 + 20 = 30
0x14    | 0x401182B3  | SUB x6, x3, x1         | x6 = 20 - 10 = 10
0x18    | 0x00409333  | SLL x7, x1, x4         | x7 = 10 << 4 = 160
0x1c    | 0x0030A3B3  | SLT x8, x1, x3         | x8 = (10<20)?1:0 = 1
0x20    | 0x0011B433  | SLTU x9, x3, x1        | x9 = (20<10)?1:0 = 0
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === R-Type逻辑测试 ===
0x24    | 0x0030C4B3  | XOR x10, x1, x3        | x10 = 10 XOR 20 = 30 (01010^10100=11110)
0x28    | 0x0041D533  | SRL x11, x3, x4        | x11 = 20 >> 4 = 1
0x2c    | 0x4041D5B3  | SRA x12, x3, x4        | x12 = 20 >> 4 = 1
0x30    | 0x0030E633  | OR x13, x1, x3         | x13 = 10 OR 20 = 30 (01010|10100=11110)
0x34    | 0x0020F6B3  | AND x14, x1, x2        | x14 = 10 AND 15 = 10 (01010&01111=01010)
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === NOP填充 ===
0x38~   | 0x00000013  | NOP (多条)             | 流水线填充

========================================
预期执行结果:
========================================
x1  = 10  (0b01010)
x2  = 15  (0b01111)
x3  = 20  (0b10100)
x4  = 4   (移位量)

R-Type算术:
x5  = 30  (ADD: 10+20)
x6  = 10  (SUB: 20-10)
x7  = 160 (SLL: 10<<4)
x8  = 1   (SLT: 10<20)
x9  = 0   (SLTU: 20<10 = false)

R-Type逻辑:
x10 = 30  (XOR: 01010^10100=11110=30)
x11 = 1   (SRL: 20>>4=1)
x12 = 1   (SRA: 20>>4=1)
x13 = 30  (OR: 01010|10100=11110=30)
x14 = 10  (AND: 01010&01111=01010=10)

========================================
二进制计算验证:
========================================
10 = 01010 (二进制)
15 = 01111 (二进制)
20 = 10100 (二进制)

10 XOR 20 = 01010 ^ 10100 = 11110 = 30 ✓
10 OR 20  = 01010 | 10100 = 11110 = 30 ✓
10 AND 15 = 01010 & 01111 = 01010 = 10 ✓

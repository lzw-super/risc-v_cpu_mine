========================================
功能: 测试所有Load/Store指令及Load-Use冒险
      LB, LH, LW, LBU, LHU, SB, SH, SW
========================================

========================================
预期执行结果:
========================================
x1  = 0x100
x2  = 0x12345678
x10 = 0x80

Word测试:
x3  = 0x12345678 (LW)

Byte测试:
x4  = 0xFFFFFF80 (LB, 有符号扩展 -128)
x5  = 0x80       (LBU, 无符号)

Halfword测试:
x6  = 0x5678     (LH, 0x5678是正数，无符号扩展)
x7  = 0x5678     (LHU)

Load-Use冒险测试:
x8  = 0x12345678
x9  = 0x2468ACF0 (x8+x8, 正确计算)

========================================
指令编码说明:
========================================
Load指令 (opcode = 0x03):
  I-Type: imm[31:20] | rs1[19:15] | funct3[14:12] | rd[11:7] | opcode[6:0]
  LB:  funct3=000
  LH:  funct3=001
  LW:  funct3=010
  LBU: funct3=100
  LHU: funct3=101

Store指令 (opcode = 0x23):
  S-Type: imm[31:25] | rs2[24:20] | rs1[19:15] | funct3[14:12] | imm[11:7] | opcode[6:0]
  SB:  funct3=000
  SH:  funct3=001
  SW:  funct3=010

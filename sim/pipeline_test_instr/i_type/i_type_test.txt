========================================
RISC-V I-Type指令测试程序
========================================
功能: 测试所有I-Type立即数算术逻辑指令
      ADDI, SLLI, SLTI, SLTIU, XORI, SRLI, SRAI, ORI, ANDI
========================================

地址    | 机器码      | 指令                    | 说明
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === 初始化 ===
0x00    | 0x06400093  | ADDI x1, x0, 100       | x1 = 100 (基础值)
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === I-Type算术测试 ===
0x04    | 0xFCE08113  | ADDI x2, x1, -50       | x2 = 50 (测试负立即数)
0x08    | 0x00209193  | SLLI x3, x1, 2         | x3 = 400 (100<<2)
0x0c    | 0x0C80A233  | SLTI x4, x1, 200       | x4 = 1 (100<200 有符号)
0x10    | 0x0320B2B3  | SLTIU x5, x1, 50       | x5 = 0 (100<50 无符号)
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === I-Type逻辑测试 ===
0x14    | 0x0FF0C433  | XORI x6, x1, 0xFF      | x6 = 155 (100 XOR 255)
0x18    | 0x003154B3  | SRLI x7, x1, 3         | x7 = 12 (100>>3)
0x1c    | 0x40215533  | SRAI x8, x1, 2         | x8 = 25 (100>>2 算术)
0x20    | 0x00F0E5B3  | ORI x9, x1, 0x0F       | x9 = 107 (100|15)
0x24    | 0x0F00F633  | ANDI x10, x1, 0xF0     | x10 = 96 (100&240)
--------|-------------|------------------------|------------------------------------------
        |             | === NOP填充 ===
0x28    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |
0x2c    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |
0x30    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |
0x34    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |
0x38    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |
0x3c    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |
0x40    | 0x00000013  | NOP                    |

========================================
预期执行结果:
========================================
x1  = 100
x2  = 50   (ADDI: 100-50)
x3  = 400  (SLLI: 100<<2)
x4  = 1    (SLTI: 100<200)
x5  = 0    (SLTIU: 100<50 unsigned false)
x6  = 155  (XORI: 100^255)
x7  = 12   (SRLI: 100>>3)
x8  = 25   (SRAI: 100>>2)
x9  = 107  (ORI: 100|15)
x10 = 96   (ANDI: 100&240)

========================================
机器码计算说明:
========================================
I-Type格式: imm[31:20] | rs1[19:15] | funct3[14:12] | rd[11:7] | opcode[6:0]
opcode = 0x13 (0010011)

ADDI:  funct3=000
SLLI:  funct3=001, imm[31:25]=0000000 (shamt在imm[24:20])
SLTI:  funct3=010
SLTIU: funct3=011
XORI:  funct3=100
SRLI:  funct3=101, imm[31:25]=0000000
SRAI:  funct3=101, imm[31:25]=0100000
ORI:   funct3=110
ANDI:  funct3=111

负立即数编码:
-50 = 12位有符号数 = 0xFCE (符号扩展到32位)
